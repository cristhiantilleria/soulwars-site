═══════════════════════════════════════
GAME OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════
Free browser BYOND MMO set in the Bleach universe. Choose 1 of 7 factions,
train stats, master skill trees, win events, and fight for spiritual dominance.

Key features:
- 7 factions: Shinigami, Hollow/Arrancar, Quincy/Wandenreich, Fullbringer, Vaizard, Bount, Sinner
- Event-driven progression (Shikai/Bankai/Resurrecciones all require event wins)
- Rebirth system for prestige SP bonuses and new faction unlocks
- 16 stat points per level (ATK/DEF/REI/DEX); Spirit Level adds +30 all stats (max 10x)
- Perk slots: 9 minor, 3 major, 1 ultimate — each with 3 ranks

v3.0 Changes (April 2026): Race skill trees (~200 nodes), Wandenreich as own faction,
Alchemy & Mining professions, Faction War Map PvP zone, Squad 0, all Quincy Schrifts added,
full balance transparency. 130k+ lines of code changed.

═══════════════════════════════════════
STATS
═══════════════════════════════════════
ATK: Normal attack damage (max/min), Normal/Precision Crit damage, player HP
DEF: Damage reduction per point, Crit damage reduction. Hard cap: 70% reduction.
REI: Reiatsu attack damage (max/min), Reiatsu Crit damage, Reiryoku & HP (smaller).
DEX: Crit hit rate, Accuracy, Avoidability.
Spirit Level: +30 all stats per level, max 10 levels (+300 total). Buy with Spirit Dust at Artemis.

Currencies: EP (events) | SD/Spirit Dust (mobs→Spirit Level, resets) | SE (PvP kills→gear)
GP (guild wars) | SP (premium cash) | Hell Points (rebirth) | PvP Souls/Hell Souls (gear leveling)

═══════════════════════════════════════
LEVELING GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════
Lv1-25 Early: Tutorial quest, Class event (~8 free levels), Delinquents→Hollows, join faction ASAP
Lv25-60 Mid: Unlock Shikai(Shin)/stay VL(Hollow), group farm, train passives, move to Hueco Mundo
Lv60-90 Late: Get 1 event win (mandatory for major unlocks), finalize stat build, Lt at Lv65
Lv90-130 End: Limit Release (Lt/Captain only), Captain rank (Bankai+Lv90+beat Captain), Ultimate perk Lv120
Rebirth: Resets Lv→1, keeps perks, +15 SP per level above 90, xEXP×2.5 after 1st RB, unlocks Sinner

Masteries: choose ONE at Lv40 — permanent specialisation.

─── SHINIGAMI ───

  - Shakkahō: Active skill: fire a concentrated sphere of reiryoku at your target.
      > Shakkahō — Rapid: Augment: casts two Shakkaho blasts (second after 0.15s)
      > Shakkahō — Blazing: Augment: applies a burn dealing 50% of Shakkaho damage over 6 seconds.
  - Sōkatsui: Active skill: unleash a wave of blue spiritual flames.
      > Sōkatsui — Slow: Augment: Sōkatsui slows enemies hit for 2 seconds with a level 1 slow.
      > Sōkatsui — Rapid: Augment: Sōkatsui casts two Sōkatsui blasts (second after 0.15s)
  - Sōren Sōkatsui: Active skill: dual-layered Sōkatsui for amplified devastation.
      > Sōren Sōkatsui — Rapid: Augment: Sōren Sōkatsui casts two Sōkatsui blasts (second after 0.15s)
      > Sōren Sōkatsui — Blazing: Augment: Sōren Sōkatsui applies a burn dealing 50% of extra damage over 6 seconds.
      > Sōren Sōkatsui — Fast: Augment: Sōren Sōkatsui travels twice as fast
  - Kurohitsugi: Active skill: Creates a black coffin around you target dealing heavy damage.
      > Kurohitsugi — Focused: Augment: Triples in size.
      > Kurohitsugi — Stunning: Augment: stuns all enemies hit for 2 seconds.
      > Kurohitsugi — Silenced: Augment: Kurohitsugi has a 30% chance to silence targets hit for 3 seconds.
  - Senju Kōten Taihō: Active skill: A cone of extremely powerful reiatsu blasts.
      > Senju Kōten Taihō — Chain Reaction: Augment: creates an explosive chain reaction, all enemies hit explode enemies within 2 tiles.
      > Senju Kōten Taihō — Flashing: Augment: the distance and movement speed are doubled.
      > Senju Kōten Taihō — Piercing: Augment: projectiles now pierce objects, mobs, and walls.
  - Ittō Kasō: Active skill: sacrifice to conjure an immense pillar of flame.
      > Ittō Kasō — Focused: Augment: triples the range of Itto Kasō catching a target.
      > Ittō Kasō — Damage: Augment: increases the damage of Itto Kasō by 50%.
      > Ittō Kasō — Wildfire: Augment: spreads an Itto Kasō to all enemies within 2 tiles of the original target.
      > Ittō Kasō — Silencing: Augment: Itto Kasō has a 30% chance to silence targets hit for 3 seconds.
  - Sekienton: Active skill: create a concealing cloud of smoke to obscure sight.
      > Sekienton — Shrouding: Augment: inside Sekienton gain +50% evasion but the cloud's opacity is reduced by 50%.
      > Sekienton — Instincts: Augment: falling below 25% HP spawns a 3s Sekienton (30s cooldown).
  - Tenran: Active skill: conjure a spiraling wind blast to knock foes back.
      > Tenran — Seiran: Augment: Tenran now has a 30% chance to silence targets hit for 3s.
  - Dankū: Active skill: raise a barrier that blocks incoming projectiles.
      > Dankū — Reflective: Augment: deflect the first projectile that hits Danku, then dissipate the barrier.
      > Dankū — Trained: Augment: doubles the width of Danku.
  - Hainawa: Active skill: restrain a target with a rope of yellow reiryoku.
  - Geki: Active skill: bind foes with crimson light to immobilize them.
      > Geki — Focused: Augment: extends Geki range by 2 tiles but increases cast time by 0.4s.
      > Geki — Potency: Augment: increases Geki stun duration by 0.75 seconds.

  Passives:
  - Wind Stance: Passive: unlock the Wind stance as a fourth Zanjutsu stance. Landing F/G reduces flash step cooldown by 0.3s
  - Wind Step: Passive: Knocks all enemies within 2 tiles of the user back. Any other shunpo effects will apply first.
  - Wind Slash: Passive: All enemies within 1 tiles are slashed when you shunpo.
  - Energy Waves: Passive: G attack launches a 1 tile wave of energy that has a 4 tile distance.
  - Piercing Waves: Passive: Energy Waves now pierce enemies and walls and has a 6 tile distance.
  - Wind Fury: Passive: Wind Slash now has a 3 tile radius.
  - Precision Hits: Passive: Critical strikes pierce 10% of defence.
  - Duelist Movement: Passive: landing an F/G attack increases movement speed by +2 for 2s. does not stack.
  - Adaptive Kidō: Passive: offensive Kido spells scale 50% with Reiatsu and 50% with of your highest stat.
  - Empowered Star Combo: Passive: Star Combo deals +20% damage (F then G slash combo).
  - Nimaiya Training: Passive: successive F/G hits deal +10% of damage as true damage.
  - Hakaze Parry: Passive: Removes the projectile parry cooldown, parry now reduces projectile damage by 40% ontop of the normal parry reduction.
  - Kidō Specialist: Passive: CC Kido gains +0.5s duration and all Kido have +10% damage

  Masteries (choose ONE at Level 40):
  - Zanjutsu Mastery [Lv40]: Damage abilities +6% damage, healing +10%, shielding +10%, costs -10%.
  - Battōjutsu Mastery [Lv40]: Landing F/G attacks grants +5% crit and +25% attack speed for 3 swings (14s CD).
  - Ryōdan Mastery [Lv40]: After 3 hits in 2s, deal ghost damage = 1.5x F attack and heal 20% (12s CD).
  - Shukuchi Mastery [Lv40]: Shunpo within 5 tiles grants +2% crit/+1% attack stacks (max 5, 15s duration).

PERK SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════
PP: 1 per 2 levels. Reset cost: 30 Spirit Dust.
Minor (9 slots, Lv10/20/40/50/70/80/90/100/110): R1=1PP R2=2PP R3=3PP
Major (3 slots, Lv30/60/90): R1=2PP R2=4PP R3=6PP cumulative
Ultimate (1 slot, Lv120): R1=5PP R2=10PP R3=15PP cumulative

MINOR PERKS:
  Subsequent Strikes [offense]: R1: every 6 hits → 25% bonus dmg | R2: every 5 hits → 25% | R3: every 4 hits → 50% (10s CD)
    Melee finisher perk. The CD resets the hit counter, so build around gap-closing.
  Critical Chance Mastery [offense]: R1: +2% | R2: +4% | R3: +6% crit chance
    Core for DEX/crit builds. Stacks with DEX stat directly.
  Critical Damage Mastery [offense]: R1: +3.3% | R2: +6.6% | R3: +9.9% crit damage
    Makes every crit hit harder. Pair with Crit Chance for maximum burst.
  Reiatsu Training [offense]: R1: +5% | R2: +10% | R3: +15% Reiatsu attack dmg
    Best perk for Kido/Cero builds. Applies to ALL reiatsu-type skills.
  Empower [offense]: R1: +4% | R2: +8% | R3: +12% to 1 core stat
    Choose your highest stat — at 380 REI, +12% = ~45 free stat points worth.
  Combat Training [offense]: R1: +5% | R2: +10% | R3: +15% dmg with Normal/Defence attacks
    Pure melee damage perk. Best for Str/Def stat builds using F/G attacks.
  Cleave [offense]: R1: 20% | R2: 40% | R3: 60% hard-attack dmg to adjacent enemies
    Hard attack (G) hits multiple targets. Excellent for mob farming and PVP crowds.
  Health Pool [defense]: R1: +5% | R2: +10% | R3: +15% max Health
    Pure survivability. Great for tank builds or low-DEF setups.
  Toughness [defense]: R1: +2% | R2: +4% | R3: +6% Defense
    Flat defense boost. Good for patching low DEF investments.
  Dodge Mastery [defense]: R1: +5% | R2: +7.5% | R3: +10% Dodge Chance
    Stacks with DEX avoidability. Good for hit-and-run playstyles.
  Regeneration Training [defense]: R1: +10% | R2: +20% | R3: +30% HP Regen
    Passive health recovery. Combos well with defensive masteries.
  Recovery [defense]: R1: 2% | R2: 4% | R3: 6% of damage taken returned over 5s
    Heals based on damage received. Resets on each new hit — useful in sustained fights.
  Energy Pool [utility]: R1: +5% | R2: +10% | R3: +15% max Reiryoku
    More Kido casts before running dry. Good for Kido-heavy builds.
  Inner Spirit [utility]: R1: +5% | R2: +10% | R3: +15% Reiryoku Regen
    Recover Reiryoku faster between fights. Pairs with Energy Pool.
  Free Shot [utility]: R1: 50% cost | R2: 20% cost | R3: 0 cost every 10s
    Free Kido cast every 10s. Great for saving Reiryoku in extended fights.
  Momentum [utility]: R1: +1 speed | R2: +2 speed | R3: +3 speed after 5s no combat
    Speed bonus while roaming. Drops on damage dealt/received.
  Surge [utility]: R1: 5% | R2: 10% | R3: 15% chance to 2x Reiryoku regen for 5s (10s CD)
    Procs on ability use. Helps sustain Kido spam in long engagements.
  Fracture ⭐ [pvp]: R1: -1.5%/stack | R2: -2.25%/stack | R3: -3%/stack DEF (max 10 stacks, 30s)
    ⭐ Best PVP perk. Max = -30% enemy DEF at R3. Every hit applies a stack.
  Bloodletting [pvp]: R1: 5s | R2: 10s | R3: 15s — target heals 50% less (20s CD)
    Counters tank/regen builds hard. Essential for end-game PVP.
  Leg Slice [pvp]: R1: 1s | R2: 1s | R3: 2s cripple on back hit (25s CD)
    Mobility control. Pairs with flash step to back-hit consistently.
  Flash Echo [pvp]: R1: 2 tiles | R2: 2 tiles | R3: 3 tiles — 20% auto-flash away (30s CD)
    Reactive escape tool. Counters dash-in aggression automatically.
  Seething Hatred [pvp]: R1: +2% | R2: +4% | R3: +6% dmg vs chosen faction
    Pick your most common opponent's faction. Free flat damage against them.
  Envenom [pvp]: R1: 5s stack | R2: 10s stack | R3: 15s — 0.25%/s target HP as poison (30s CD)
    DoT perk scaling off target's max HP. Strong against high-HP tanks.
  Fake Out [pvp]: R1: 1s clone | R2: 2s clone | R3: 3s — go invisible + decoy on dodge (10s CD)
    Procs on successful dodge. Great for repositioning or resetting a fight.
  Reaper [farming]: R1: +10% | R2: +20% | R3: +30% Spirit Dust drop rate
    Farming perk. Speeds up Spirit Level progression significantly.
  Brilliance [farming]: R1: +10% | R2: +20% | R3: +30% experience gained
    Leveling perk. Excellent for early game or after rebirth.
  Lucky Find [farming]: R1: 5% → 150% EXP | R2: 5% → 200% | R3: 10% chance 200% bonus EXP
    Random EXP spikes from kills. Pairs with Brilliance for leveling builds.

MAJOR PERKS:
  Shock ⭐ [pvp]: R1: 5% chance | R2: 10% chance | R3: 15% chance to Silence 3s (25s CD)
    ⭐ Top major perk. Silence prevents enemy skills mid-fight. Procs on every hit.
  Chill [pvp]: R1: 10% | R2: 15% | R3: 20% chance to Slow 2s (25s CD)
    Higher proc than Shock. Slows movement — great for kiting.
  Ignite ⭐ [offense]: R1: 5% | R2: 10% | R3: 15% damage as burn over 5s (no stack)
    ⭐ Procs on EVERY hit. Kido combos trigger it repeatedly for massive burn.
  Evasion [defense]: R1: +15% | R2: +25% | R3: +35% dodge when below 26% HP
    Clutch survival tool. At low HP you become very hard to hit.
  Flash Pressure [pvp]: R1: 3 tiles | R2: 4 tiles | R3: 5 tile AoE on flash step — slow + ground 5s (45s CD)
    Turn your flash step into crowd control. Excellent for aggressive melee.
  Last Grasp [defense]: R1: 5 tiles | R2: 7 tiles | R3: 9 tile AoE when below 30% HP — slow + ground (45s CD)
    Defensive panic button. Drops below 30% HP → enemy team gets grounded.
  Vampiric Aura [defense]: R1: 2.5% | R2: 5% | R3: 7.5% damage converted to lifesteal
    Passive sustain on every hit. Scales well with high DPS builds.
  Flurry Strikes [offense]: R1: -15% | R2: -30% | R3: -45% F/G attack speed
    Massive attack speed boost. Best major for melee builds.

ULTIMATE PERKS:
  Berserker's Rage ⭐ [offense]: R1: +0.16%/1% missing HP | R2: +0.33% | R3: +0.5% dmg per 1% missing HP
    At 30% HP → +35% dmg. At 10% HP → +45% dmg. Best ultimate for offensive builds.
  Stand Behind Me [defense]: R1: +0.16%/1% missing HP | R2: +0.33% | R3: +0.5% Defense per 1% missing HP
    Mirror of Berserker's Rage but for DEF. Excellent for tank builds at low HP.

  Lv110 (minor): Free Shot R3 — free ability cost every 10s
  Lv120 (ultimate): Berserker's Rage R3 (offense) or Stand Behind Me R3 (tank)

NEW PLAYER TIPS
═══════════════════════════════════════
1. Tutorial Quest (Teacher→Pool→15 Delinquents→Chef→Urahara candy) — free EXP
2. Class event active? Idle in classroom north of spawn for ~8 free levels

4. Train passives: hit dummies non-spiky side, or use a bumper buddy
5. Hollow: stay Vasto Lorde before Arrancar for faster passive training

7. ATK release → ATK+DEF build. REI release → REI+DEF build.

9. Kill Quest NPC near your grind spot — bonus EXP every session
